About me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Statistical Sciences at Sapienza University of Rome supervised by Prof. Lavinia Amorosi I hold a Master’s degree in Statistical Sciences from Sapienza University of Rome and a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from the University of Salento.

My academic interests lie at the intersection of Operational Research and Statistics. My research mainly focuses on multi-objective discrete optimization (MODO) problems and mathematical optimization for statistics and machine learning.

Research areas: Operational Research, Statistics.

Research Interests

  • Multi-Objective Optimization
  • Discrete Optimization Problems
  • Operational Research for Statistics and Machine Learning
