About me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Statistical Sciences at Sapienza University of Rome. I hold a Master’s degree in Statistical Sciences from Sapienza University of Rome and a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from the University of Salento. My academic interests lie at the intersection of Operational Research and Statistics.

My research mainly focuses on multi-objective discrete optimization (MODO) problems and mathemat- ical optimization for statistics and machine learning.

Research areas: Operational research, Statistics.

Research interests

  • Multi-Objective Optimization
  • Discrete problems
  • Operational Research for Statistics
